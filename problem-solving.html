Problem Solving

Times where I was:

1. blocked on a simple problem:

I got stuck a few times on very simple errors, like syntax within the code being slightly wrong and it would take me a long time to figure out the small issue.

what problem-solving techniques did you use?

I took a step back to look at the bigger picture and figure out what in the code was wrong. Eventually I looked on google to find what I needed.

how did you feel throughout the process?

I was frustrated that such small errors could be such a big setback and relieved when I finally solved the issue.

what did you learn?
sometimes stepping back to look at the bigger picture of the code can help identify errors.

2. elegantly solved a problem.

what was the problem?

Using the same way of problem solving as last time I had some issues with how parts of my code were performing. I stepped back and took a look at the bigger picture to see what was wrong and how to adjust it.

how did you feel throughout the process?

I felt stepping away made me feel a little more collected about how I was looking at the problem

what did you learn?

Changing perspectives can help understand code.
