Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long term goal is to apply the knowledge from my time at Dev Academy and get a job in the development scene, eventually reaching a point where I can afford to live comfortably and take care of my family.

My strengths in learning:

Tenacity, I will keep persisting towards a goal even if I struggle or take a long time to do it. I see failure as an opportunity to grow and improve. Willingness to learn new things, I love new information and learning anything new is always rewarding.

Weaknesses in learning:

I never feel as though what I have done is enough, even if I put my best into something I regularly struggle with feeling my work is inadeqate.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

The thing I would like to learn most is management of time and effort. At the moment I struggle to apply even amounts of effort over a period of time. I tend to get a lot of my work done in short bursts. The issue with this method of working is that sometimes that energy isn't there and I find myself having to really push to get anything done. Ideally I would like to learn to overcome this and either learn to manage my style of learning better or find another way to work.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you? A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will manage myself by setting a block of time each day aside for work each day rather than spontaneous bursts of effort. This will help with consistency of my own work and improve the quality. I will maintain communication with the course facilitators and others in my cohort When things build up and it starts to emotionally effect me, I like to go talk to someone about it, whether that be a facilitator within dev academy or a friend or family member, sometimes all that it takes to feel better when things get overwhelming is to stop and talk. I have my homegroup and group facilitator to talk to on discord should anything go wrong.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

My expectations for the facilitation team are to help when help is needed and to be kind and patient when doing so.

I have no other commitments outside of Dev Academy (ideally this means all my work gets done asap)
